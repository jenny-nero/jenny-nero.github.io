WHO, WHEN, WHERE, HOW?
The Arerecibo Message was created by scientists including Frank Drake and Carl Sagan. It was sent on November 16, 1974 from the Arecibo Observatory in Puerto Rico.
The message was transmitted --> toward the Messier 13, about 25,000 light-years away...
It used a powerful radio signal (1679 binary bits) arranged as a visual grid encoding basic information about humanity.
The content included numbers, DNA structure, human form, Earth’s position, and the Solar System, designed to be understood by intelligent extraterrestrial life.
Rather than expecting a reply, the message was mainly a symbolic demonstration of human technological capability and a celebration of the upgraded telescope...
“The sending of this message was largely a ceremonial event...
a demonstration that

WE COULD DO IT.”


Carl Sagan

what is purpose.
NOT reply, but declaration: we exist, WE CAN ENCODE OURSELVES, we can be understood—even if no one is listening. The Arecibo Message was not meant to start a conversation but to stage a declaration. Its purpose was to translate humanity into a universal, minimal language—mathematics—capable of crossing the unknown. It compresses identity into logic, presenting an ordered version of life on Earth.

More than communication, it acts as a symbolic gesture: a broadcast of preresence, intelligence, and cuririosity into space.
